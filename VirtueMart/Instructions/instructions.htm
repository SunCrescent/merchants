Step 1) In the Joomla Administration site, go to the "Extensions" menu item, and select "Extension Manager".

Step 2) Use the "Browse" button to locate the "CaVirtEx Payment Plugin.tar.gz" file on your computer, then click "Upload & Install".

Step 3) Go to the "Extensions" menu item again, and this time select the "Plug-in Manager" menu item.

Step 4) Find the "CaVirtEx Bitcoin Plugin" in the list, then click on the red "Status" icon to enable the plug-in. Make sure to write down your plug-in's "ID" (from the last column).

Step 5) Now go to the "Components" menu item, and click on the "VirtueMart" option.

Step 6) Click on the "Shop" menu item, then click on "Payment Methods".

Step 7) Click on the "New" button.

Step 8) Enter "Bitcoins" as the Payment Name, "Pay quickly and easily using BTC." as the Payment Description, and select "CaVirtEx Bitcoin Plugin" as the Payment Method. Then, click "Save".

Step 9) Click on "Configuration".

Step 10) Enter the Merchant Key and Secret Key you'll find on the CaVirtEx website. Click on "Save & Close".

Step 11) On the CaVirtEx site, click "Merchant Profile". Find IPN URL, and enter the URL for your Joomla index.php file with the following query string:

http://[your domain]/[your joomla folder, if any]/index.php?option=com_virtuemart&view=pluginresponse&task=pluginnotification&tmpl=component&pm=[your plug-in ID]

e.g. http://aaronk.ca/joomla/index.php?option=com_virtuemart&view=pluginresponse&task=pluginnotification&tmpl=component&pm=10032

You can now accept payments with bitcoins!
